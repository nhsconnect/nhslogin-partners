How it works v0 v1

NHS login is an internet facing system for identifying and verifying a user’s identity.

It uses the Open ID Connect and the Oauth2.0 protocols. It also uses FIDO (Fast Identity Online) specifications which improves the user’s experience by allowing Fingerprint ID and Face ID seamlessly access native apps.

NHS login offers different levels of verification so you can choose the most appropriate for your service. To determine the right level of verification, review the guidance within the standards to understand the policy for the transactions within your service.

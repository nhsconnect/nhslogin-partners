Why use NHS login?

If you’re building a health or social care app or website for patients, NHS login is a flexible solution which can take away the headache of creating an account for a user, verifying the users’ identity and linking it to an NHS number.

Benefits for your users

  • One-time simple Sign-up process
  • Single login to access multiple Health and Social care services
  • Peace of mind their data is secure

Benefits for your organisation

  • Being sure your users are who they say they are
  • Not having to build and manage an identity platform
  • It’s free

Find out if NHS login is right for your service

How it works

NHS login is an internet facing system for identifying and verifying a user’s identity using Open ID Connect and the Oauth2.0 protocols alongside FIDO (Fast Identity Online) specifications for improving the user’s experience of accessing services.

Safety and security

Proving users’ identities using the NHS login meets the published standard for identity checking in health and care services. Read about the standards on proving identity that NHS login follows.

NHS login stores and processes users’ personal information securely and in line with strict standards. Read more about data storage.

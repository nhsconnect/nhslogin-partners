Before you request onboarding

1. Check if you need NHS login

Before you start connecting your service to NHS login, find out how NHS login works and if it’s right for your service.

2. Choose your ‘Vector of Trust'

You will need to determine the ‘Vector of Trust’ for your service. This is the minimum level of user verification that you should be willing to accept before providing a user with access to your service.

As part of the assurance process we’ll ask that you’ve gone through this process.

3. Develop in the Sandpit

The Sandpit is a copy of our production code environment which will help you learn how to integrate with our service. Developing in the Sandpit is a mandatory step before a service can be onboarded.

The Sandpit can be accessed from the NHS login GitHub.

4. Begin preparation for ‘Supplier conformance assessment’

We have started the journey to standardise the way we assure partners across NHS digital. The aim is to reduce the duplication but we’re not there yet. This means you’ll need to review the NHS login Hazard Log and Safety Case. You’ll also need to have a recent Data Security and Protection Toolkit at the time you go live.

If you are new to working with NHS Digital you will need support arrangements set up. We’ll offer early life support how much and for how long? Your ongoing support will be via a help desk to help desk model.

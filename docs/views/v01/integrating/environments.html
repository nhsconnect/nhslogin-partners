Use our stub to learn about OpenID Connect

Our stub is a lightweight representation of our OpenID Connect (OIDC) implementation. It is intended to provide rapid learning of how our OIDC authorization flow behaves.

You can start using this now: https://stub.signin.nhs.uk/client

Try out NHS login in our sandpit

The sandpit environment will help you learn how to integrate with our service. However, it doesn't offer a full experience. The key restrictions are:

  • It is not possible to complete submitted ID verification checks, only a basic registration
  • Its not connected to the Personal Demographics Service (PDS)

How to onboard

1. Request to be prioritised for onboarding

To begin the process you must first send an onboarding request email to engage.nhslogin@nhs.net

2. Complete and return the ‘scoring spreadsheet’

The scoring spreadsheet allows us to prioritise your service against others wanting to join the beta. Prioritisation will be based on a variety of factors such as strategic fit and benefits to health and social care.

3. Provide usage forecasts

Once your service has been prioritised, we will ask you to provide usage forecasts so we ensure we can meet those demands during beta.

4. Complete ‘Supplier Conformance Assessment’

We will ask you to complete a ‘Supplier Conformance Assessment’. This will confirm that you have completed a ‘Data protection impact assessment’, and are adhering to the required standards for Information Governance, Security and Clinical safety.

We’ll also require you to complete end-to-end testing in the assurance environment, including successful redirect back to the onboarding service

5. Set up Support with NHS login

NHS login is supported by the National Service Desk. You’ll need to be registered with the service desk in order to raise support tickets to us. Find out more here add correct link.

6. Sign the connection agreement

Subject to passing the assurance standard, and successfully developing within the assurance environment, we will ask you to sign a connection agreement with NHS Digital.

7. Obtain signed declarations from your end user organisations

We’ll also require each of your end user organisations to sign a declaration.

Once these steps are complete you can go live.

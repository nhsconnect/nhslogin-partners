Why use NHS login?

If you’re building a health or social care app or website for patients, NHS login is a free, flexible solution which can take away the headache of creating an account for a user, verifying the user’s identity, and linking it to their NHS number.

Is it right for your service?

NHS login is designed and funded for Health and Social Care services. It provides user verification facilities and the creation of a user account that can be used to login to other Health and Social Care Services.

If your service aligns to NHS strategy and delivers benefits to the NHS and social care ecosystem you can apply to use NHS login to check a user’s identity.

Your service might need to do this if it:

  • shows a user personal information about themselves, such as their medical records
  • replaces a form that needs to be physically signed by a user

The team behind NHS login work in a user-centred, agile way. As such, the service will be continually improved via iterative releases that have been validated via user research and testing.
